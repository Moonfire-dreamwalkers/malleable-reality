Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best spiritual Discord servers?

The best spiritual Discord servers include Malleable Reality (simulation theory and the Great Work), Sanctum Hermeticum (7,000+ members exploring hermetic philosophy), ThelemaCord (Thelema and ceremonial magick), Astral Society (astral projection and lucid dreaming), and Gnostic Gospels (Nag Hammadi study). This directory lists 31 active communities curated for quality and engagement.

How do I join an occult Discord community?

Click any invite link in this directory. You'll be taken directly to the server's Discord invite page — you just need a free Discord account. Most servers are open to all experience levels, from total beginners exploring witchcraft or Thelema for the first time, to advanced practitioners of ceremonial magick and Left-Hand Path traditions.

Is there a Discord for witchcraft beginners?

Yes — All Things Spiritual, Witchy Study Buddies, Spellbound, and Fate Weavers all welcome witchcraft beginners. They offer resource libraries, study channels, and mentorship. Fate Weavers specifically includes 90+ practice roles to help you find your path within the craft.

What is the largest spiritual Discord server?

Sanctum Hermeticum is one of the largest occult Discord servers with 7,000+ members exploring hermetic philosophy and occult history. ThelemaCord is among the largest communities focused specifically on Aleister Crowley's Thelema. Malleable Reality leads this network as the top featured community.

Are there Discord servers for astral projection?

Astral Society specializes in astral projection, lucid dreaming, and metaphysical expansion. The 5th Dimension server covers consciousness exploration and non-duality. Both are active communities with experienced guides and shared resources for those seeking to explore beyond the physical.
