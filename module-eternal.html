The Eternal Frame

The universe functions through a series of logically linked frames that strictly avoid any type of null or end state. This mechanism ensures that existence remains a continuous flow of information where every potential frame must maintain a high level of entropic logical flow to even be rendered.

If a specific timeline or event would result in a complete collapse of logic or a total termination of consciousness it simply will not manifest within the observer's experience.

The Coherence Filter

Reality is inherently constrained by this need for coherence which acts as a fundamental filter for what can actually happen.

Because the universe will not allow a frame to emerge if it results in a direct continuity conflict, your path is always being funneled toward the most integrated and stable outcomes. This preservation of integrity means that the universe is essentially a self-correcting web of interconnected systems that prioritizes the maintenance of a functional and observable reality above all else.

Quantum Immortality

Quantum immortality is the natural byproduct of this system because an observer cannot experience a frame where their own consciousness has reached a definitive end point.

Since the death frame lacks the necessary logical continuity for the individual to perceive it, the universe instead shifts focus to the next available coherent frame. You are always being carried forward by the requirement for entropic flow, which guarantees that your journey through the various layers of consciousness will always find a way to persist.
